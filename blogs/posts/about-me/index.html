Hi, I’m Onkar Bhardwaj, a passionate technologist, researcher, and leader with expertise in Artificial Intelligence, distributed systems, and high-performance computing. Currently, I lead the AI Models Software Group at the MIT-IBM Watson AI Lab, where I focus on foundational AI models, their practical applications, and their integration into impactful software systems.

My journey spans diverse experiences—from advancing generative AI tools and creating scalable machine learning frameworks to developing defense strategies for distributed systems at Akamai and contributing to high-performance computing during my time at IBM and academia. Along the way, I’ve worked on cutting-edge technologies, published in top-tier conferences like NeurIPS and ICSE, and mentored cross-functional teams to solve complex problems.

With a Ph.D. in Electrical Engineering and Computer Science from Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute and a Master’s from IISc Bangalore, I thrive at the intersection of research and real-world impact. Whether it’s crafting AI solutions for industries like finance and construction or optimizing distributed systems serving billions of users daily, I love turning innovative ideas into reality.

When I’m not engineering solutions or mentoring teams, you can find me exploring the latest in computational creativity or tinkering with dynamic web applications. Let’s connect and discuss how AI and technology can shape the future!
